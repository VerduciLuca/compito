The rise of AI generated content

AI means artificial intelligence, which is the ability of a computer or a machine to think or act in a more human way. AI can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as understanding speech, recognizing images, or playing games

AI can generate any type of content, however it has some challenges and limitations. It needs human editing to ensure quality and accuracy. It raises ethical concerns about authenticity and ownership. It may cause plagiarism issues if it copies existing content. AI-generated content should be used with caution and care.
